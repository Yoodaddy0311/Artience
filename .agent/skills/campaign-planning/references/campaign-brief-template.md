# Campaign Brief Template

**Structured Campaign Definition**: Complete brief ensures alignment across teams, measurable execution, and post-campaign analysis.

## Core Campaign Brief Structure

| Section | Required Fields | Example Content | Validation |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Campaign Name** | Code name + full title | LAUNCH-2026-Q1: Product launch go-to-market | Unique, trackable |
| **Objective** | Business goal in SMART format | Acquire 500 free trial signups in 30 days | Measurable, time-bound |
| **Target Audience** | Persona(s), job title, pain point | Startup CTOs, <50 employees, hiring challenges | Specific, not "everyone" |
| **Key Message** | 1-2 core messages for audience | "Deploy AI teams in hours, not weeks" | Resonates with persona pain |
| **Channels** | Primary 2-3 channels by priority | 1) LinkedIn ads, 2) DevTools newsletter, 3) HN | Audience-matched, not guessed |
| **Budget** | Total spend by channel | $8K total: $4K LinkedIn, $2K newsletters, $2K content | Aligned with channel capacity |
| **Timeline** | Start/end dates, key milestones | Week 1: Creative, Week 2-3: Launch, Week 4: Optimize | Realistic execution window |
| **KPIs** | 2-3 primary metrics with targets | CTR 3%, CPC $2.50, Conversion rate 5% | Data-driven, achievable |
| **Success Criteria** | Quantified success definition | â‰¥450 signups OR CPL <$18 | Clear go/no-go threshold |
| **Post-Campaign Analysis** | How results will be reviewed | Win-loss interviews, funnel analysis, ROI attribution | Defines learning loop |

## UTM Naming Convention (Google Analytics Tracking)

| Parameter | Format | Examples | Rules |
|-----------|--------|----------|-------|
| **utm_source** | Platform sending traffic | linkedin, newsletter, hackernews | Always include |
| **utm_medium** | Traffic type | cpc, email, social, organic | Standard GA convention |
| **utm_campaign** | Campaign name | launch-q1-2026, product-webinar | Matches brief campaign name |
| **utm_content** | Ad/message variant | cta-button-v1, headline-pain, carousel-2 | Track A/B test variants |
| **utm_term** | Keywords (paid) | "ai agents", "orchestration" | For paid search only |

**Example Full URL**: `https://example.com/?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=launch-q1-2026&utm_content=cta-button-v1`

## Campaign Brief Checklist

- [ ] Objective written in SMART format (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- [ ] Target audience persona documented with research citation
- [ ] Budget allocated by channel with expected ROI or payback period
- [ ] Timeline includes all dependencies and realistic lead times
- [ ] UTM parameters defined for all trackable links
- [ ] KPIs tied to business objective with measurement plan
- [ ] Approval from marketing lead, product, and finance
- [ ] Post-launch analysis plan documented (owner + date)
