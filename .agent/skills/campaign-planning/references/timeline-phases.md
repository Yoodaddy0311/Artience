# Campaign Timeline Phases

**5-Phase Execution Framework**: Planning → Pre-launch → Launch → Sustain → Analysis structure ensures quality execution, market feedback capture, and measurable optimization.

## Campaign Phase Breakdown

| Phase | Duration | Activities | Budget % | KPIs | Owner |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------|------|-------|
| **Planning** | 2-4 weeks | Audience research, messaging workshop, channel strategy, creative brief, approval | 5% | Brief completion, team alignment | Marketing lead |
| **Pre-Launch** | 1-2 weeks | Creative production, landing page setup, ad account setup, email list prep, analytics config | 10% | Assets ready, tracking configured | Creative + ops |
| **Launch** | 1-2 weeks | Ads go live, email sends, social scheduling, press outreach, influencer outreach | 25% | Impression volume, early engagement, ad spend pace | Campaign manager |
| **Sustain** | 4-8 weeks | Monitor performance, A/B test variants, optimize bids/creative, customer support, content updates | 50% | CTR >baseline, CPC <target, Conversion rate trend | Optimization specialist |
| **Analysis** | 1-2 weeks | Full funnel analysis, win-loss interviews, ROI attribution, learning documentation, final reporting | 10% | Campaign ROI, cost per acquisition, pipeline influence | Analyst |

## Channel Coordination Matrix

| Channel | Pre-Launch Prep | Launch Actions | Optimization Levers | Reporting Metrics |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Paid Ads** (LinkedIn/Google) | Audience segmentation, creative variants (3+), landing page | Budget allocation, bid adjustment, ad scheduling | Bid strategy, audience refinement, creative rotation | CTR, CPC, Conv. rate |
| **Email** | Segmentation, template design, preview tests | Deployment timing (Tue-Thu best), subject line test | Send time optimization, content refresh, re-engagement | Open rate, CTR, conversion |
| **Content** (Blog/SEO) | Topic research, outline, keyword targeting | Publish schedule, internal linking, social distribution | Headline testing, content format mix | Organic traffic, engagement |
| **Social Organic** | Content calendar (2 weeks ahead), image/video production | Daily posting, influencer follow-ups | Hashtag testing, posting time, format (carousel vs. video) | Reach, engagement rate |
| **Partnerships** | Partner identification, agreement terms, co-marketing plan | Co-host webinar, sponsor announcement, newsletter placement | Follow-up nurture, retargeting partner audiences | Impressions, qualified leads |

## Phase Dependencies and Timing

```
Planning (Week 1-4)
    ↓
Pre-Launch (Week 5-6)
    ├─ Creative production
    ├─ Landing page setup
    └─ Analytics configuration
    ↓
Launch (Week 7-8)
    ├─ Paid ads live
    ├─ Email deployment
    └─ Social amplification
    ↓
Sustain (Week 9-16)
    ├─ Daily monitoring
    ├─ A/B test results
    └─ Performance optimization
    ↓
Analysis (Week 17-18)
    ├─ Funnel analysis
    ├─ Win-loss interviews
    └─ Final ROI attribution
```

## Timeline Validation Checklist

- [ ] Planning phase includes buyer interviews (not assumption-driven)
- [ ] Creative review completed with stakeholder sign-off
- [ ] Landing page tested for conversions in pre-launch
- [ ] Tracking pixels/UTM parameters verified before launch
- [ ] Sustain phase owner and escalation process defined
- [ ] Analysis plan includes both quantitative (metrics) and qualitative (interviews)
- [ ] Resource availability confirmed for entire timeline
- [ ] Risk contingencies identified (platform changes, resource gaps)
