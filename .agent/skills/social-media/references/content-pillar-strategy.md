# Content Pillar Strategy

**Core: Balance content mix — Educational 40%, Engaging 25%, Promotional 20%, Entertaining 10%, UGC 5%.**

## Content Mix Framework

| Pillar | Share | Content Types | Goal | Primary KPI |
|--------|-------|---------------|------|-------------|
| **Educational** | 40% | How-tos, tutorials, tips, infographics, threads | Build authority & trust | Saves, Shares |
| **Engaging** | 25% | Polls, questions, discussions, behind-scenes | Community building | Comments, Replies |
| **Promotional** | 20% | Product features, case studies, launches, offers | Drive conversions | Clicks, Conversions |
| **Entertaining** | 10% | Memes, trends, humor, culture references | Reach & virality | Shares, Impressions |
| **UGC** | 5% | Customer stories, reviews, community highlights | Social proof | Engagement Rate |

## Content Repurposing Matrix

| Source Format | Twitter/X | LinkedIn | Instagram | TikTok | Email |
|--------------|-----------|----------|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Blog Post** | Thread (5-10 tweets) | Article summary | Carousel (8-10 slides) | Key takeaway video | Newsletter section |
| **Webinar** | Quote clips | Full replay + summary | Highlight Reels | 3-5 short clips | Recap + replay link |
| **Case Study** | Stat highlights | Detailed post | Before/After visual | Customer testimonial | Featured story |
| **Report/Data** | Key stat graphics | Data visualization | Infographic | Data reveal video | Executive summary |
| **Podcast** | Audiogram clips | Guest quote post | Quote cards | Best moment clips | Episode recap |

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] 5 content pillars defined with clear ratios
- [ ] 4-week content calendar drafted
- [ ] Repurposing workflow documented (1 source → 5+ assets)
- [ ] Brand voice consistent across all pillars
- [ ] Each post maps to one pillar and one KPI
- [ ] Monthly pillar ratio audit (actual vs target)
- [ ] Quarterly pillar performance review
- [ ] Top-performing content identified for repurposing
- [ ] Content bank maintained (30+ posts queued)
- [ ] Team roles assigned: create, review, schedule, engage
