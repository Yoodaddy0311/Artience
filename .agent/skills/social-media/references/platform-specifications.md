# Platform Specifications

**Core: Each platform has unique constraints — optimize natively, never cross-post identical content.**

## Platform Technical Specs

| Platform | Post Length | Image Size | Video Length | Hashtags | Best Times (UTC) |
|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Twitter/X** | 280 chars | 1200×675px | 2:20 max | 1-3 | Tue-Thu 9-11 AM |
| **LinkedIn** | 3,000 chars | 1200×627px | 10 min | 3-5 | Tue-Thu 7-8 AM, 12 PM |
| **Instagram Feed** | 2,200 chars | 1080×1080px | 60s (90s Reels) | 5-10 | Mon-Fri 11 AM-1 PM |
| **Instagram Stories** | Overlay text | 1080×1920px | 15s per slide | 1-3 | 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM |
| **TikTok** | 2,200 chars | 1080×1920px | 10 min (60s optimal) | 3-5 | Tue-Thu 7 PM |
| **Facebook** | 63,206 chars | 1200×630px | 240 min | 1-2 | Wed-Fri 1-3 PM |
| **YouTube** | 5,000 desc | 1280×720 thumb | No limit (8-12 min ideal) | Tags: 5-8 | Sat-Sun 9-11 AM |

## Posting Frequency Guide

| Platform | Minimum | Recommended | Aggressive | Diminishing Returns |
|----------|---------|-------------|------------|---------------------|
| **Twitter/X** | 1/day | 3-5/day | 10+/day | >15/day |
| **LinkedIn** | 2/week | 3-5/week | 1-2/day | >2/day |
| **Instagram** | 3/week | 5-7/week + daily Stories | 2/day + 5 Stories | >3 posts/day |
| **TikTok** | 3/week | 1-3/day | 3-5/day | >5/day |
| **Facebook** | 2/week | 3-5/week | 1/day | >2/day |
| **YouTube** | 1/week | 2-3/week | Daily | >1/day (quality drops) |

## Hashtag Strategy

| Volume Tier | Count | Purpose | Example |
|------------|-------|---------|---------|
| **Broad** (100K+) | 1-2 | Discovery, trending | #Marketing, #SEO |
| **Medium** (10K-100K) | 3-4 | Category reach | #ContentStrategy, #B2BSaaS |
| **Niche** (<10K) | 2-3 | Targeted community | #SaaSGrowthHacks |
| **Branded** | 1 | Campaign tracking | #YourBrandName |

## Checklist

- [ ] Content resized per platform (never stretch/crop)
- [ ] Character limits verified before posting
- [ ] Hashtag mix: broad + medium + niche + branded
- [ ] Video has captions/subtitles (85% watch muted)
- [ ] Alt text on all images for accessibility
- [ ] Link in bio updated if directing traffic
- [ ] Posted within platform best-time window
- [ ] Engagement monitored first 60 min (algorithm boost)
